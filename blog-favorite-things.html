Graphics & Data Viz

Some data visualizations I think are important, novel, or inspiring.

Title Source Comments
Arctic Sea Ice Figures Zachary Labe Some of the best climate graphics on the Web. See @Zlabe on Twitter for even more.
Fundamentals of Data Visualization Claus O. Wilke I’m happy to own a hardcopy of this text, but it’s online as well. The most comprehensive text on static figures that I know of.
An Extremely Detailed Map of the 2016 Election The New York Times A much criticized graphic but incredibly fun to explore nonetheless.
There Are Many Ways to Map Election Results. We’ve Tried Most of Them. The New York Times Required reading before any U.S. election.
